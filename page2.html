Emotional Granularity

The second part of my project deals with emotional granularity. Emotional granularity is the ability to identify different emotions. As someone who is active in some online communities, I was curious if people have been increasing their range of emotions due to the digital age. Many phrases and various slang have arose from the existence of the internet and online communities and people are finding more and more ways to express themselves and their emotions. As stated before, I am fairly active in some online communities. So, for this portion of my project I have composed a list of phrases, "words", and other slang that I've seen people use online to express strong emotions. Ultimately, I want to learn if the digital age has broadened the range of emotions people can experience with new phrases and slang.

Phrases & "Words"

"The kind of tired sleep won't fix"
The feeling of depression and/or burn out.
"Screaming, crying, throwing up"
The feeling of wanting to bash your head into a wall.
"I am a genuine threat to society"
Extremely intense excitement to the point where someone is unable to sit still. Hyperness caused by excitement.
"Ough/Whoagh"
Deep admiration or appreciation or awe for something.
"Neat/Cool/Aight"
A go-with-the-flow and/or "it is what it is" submissive acceptance of something.
"It really do be like that sometimes"
A go-with-the-flow and/or "it is what it is" submissive acceptance of something.
"Greebled"
The feeling when someone's mind is racing and overwhelmed.
"Hyperfixfucked"
The feeling of heavy and passionate investment in a media. The feeling of enjoyment of something at a much higher degree than most people.
"Whablarghlism"
The feeling of heavy and passionate investment in a media. The feeling of enjoyment of something at a much higher degree than most people.
"*Keysmashing*"
Key smashing is very interesting because it is used to cover a very wide range of emotions that people experience.
"Panic Outrage Mode"
Rage and tension caused specifically by political issues or events.
"Un-fucking-believable"
Very strong disbelief and feeling of doubt.
"Screeching and tearing my hair out"
"It pulled my heartstrings" type of feeling or the feeling of wanting to bash your head into a wall.
"hhhhhh"
The feeling of being dead inside and sick of life, but not wanting to actually do anything about it. Used often for complaining.
"I am living right now."
A sarcastic expression of being in a low mood due to outside circumstances.
