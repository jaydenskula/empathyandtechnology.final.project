Depression and Its Relationship to Empathy

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest

The number of people diagnosed with depression has been rising steadily the past few years.

Research has demonstrated the potential effect that depression may have on empathy and vice versa.

Depression appears to affect empathy in two distinct ways.
